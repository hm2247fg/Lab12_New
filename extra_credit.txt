Optional (extra credit) question answer:

What other URLs strings should be used when checking that invalid YouTube URLs are rejected?

Answer:

1. Invalid YouTube video URLs:

URLs with incorrect video IDs.
URLs with invalid characters in the video ID.
URLs with incomplete video IDs.
URLs with non-existent video IDs.

2. Non-YouTube URLs:

URLs from other video hosting platforms.
URLs with incorrect formats (e.g., missing protocol, incorrect domain).
URLs to non-video content.

3. Malformed or misspelled URLs:

URLs with missing or incorrect parts (e.g., missing protocol, missing domain).
URLs with extra parameters or query strings that are not part of the standard YouTube URL format.
